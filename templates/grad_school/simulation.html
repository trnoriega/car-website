Simulating the system

Because a lot of dynamic parameters can influence how signal recognition particles interact with ribosomes, I wrote scripts based on stochastic simulation algorithms to simulate what experiments would look like when different parameters were manipulated.

For example, this image shows simulated signal recognition particle (SRP)-ribosome interactions (green line, 0 is no interaction, 1 is interaction) that depend on 5 simultaneous processes (blue solid and dashed lines, red solid and dashed lines, and black dashed line):

This allowed me to run thousands of different simulations to understand what parameters were contributing most significantly to my experimental signal and statistical analyses.
